Support Hub

Does this require rewiring?

No, our G-Switch modules are designed for retrofit. They fit behind your existing switchboards without any structural changes.

Does it work without internet?

Yes, basic local control works via physical switches. However, app control and voice commands require WiFi.

What happens if power goes out?

Our devices have memory retention. When power returns, they restore to their last state (or OFF, based on settings).
